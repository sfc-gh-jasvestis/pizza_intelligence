## Key Insights from January 6 Chicago Reviews

### Thin-Crust Decline Pattern
- 11 mentions of thin-crust quality issues across Chicago stores
- 4 explicit mentions of competitor "Crispy Crust Co."
- Common complaints: soggy, not crispy enough, cold on delivery

### Delivery Delays
- I-90 construction mentioned in 8 reviews
- Average delivery delay complaints: 20-40 minutes
- Evening deliveries (5-8pm) most affected

### Competitor Threat
- "Crispy Crust Co." mentioned 6 times
- 3 customers explicitly stated they're switching
- Competitor strength: crispier thin crust, lower prices

### Recommended Immediate Actions
1. **Operations:** Adjust delivery routes to avoid I-90 during peak hours
2. **Kitchen:** Review thin-crust prep procedure - potential quality control issue
3. **Marketing:** Counter Crispy Crust threat with promotion
4. **Staffing:** Add extra riders during 5-8pm window
